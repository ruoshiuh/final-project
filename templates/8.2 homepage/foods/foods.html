Perl milk tea

The best drink ever. This is so popular in Taiwan that it has become a category of drinks which are topped with starchy balls made from flour, or jelly.

Hotpot

This dish comes with a variety of broths, including chicken, pork, and spicy one. And we can choose which ingredients we want to cook with, be it meat, vegetable, or sometimes seafood, and the optimal times to put them into the broth.

Stinky Tofu

Many people might be put off by the smell of this dish, but it's similar to the concept of blue cheese — those who love it are absolutely passionate about it. And for me, it's an essential part of my life!

Douhua

This creamy dish, made from silky smooth soy beans, is the perfect after-meal treat. Top it with chewy tapioca balls, peanuts, and a spoonful of warm, steamed oatmeal, or anything you want.

Enjoy it curled up on the couch with your favorite TV show or movie - the simple sweetness and comforting warmth are sure to hit the spot.
